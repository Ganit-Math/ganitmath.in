Welcome To Ganit Math

We are pleased to introduce Ganit Math to you. It was introduced in January 2015 at St. Xavier's Senior Secondary School, 4 Raj Niwas Marg, Delhi, 110054. The goal of Ganit Math is to spread mathematics among the nation's smartest and most talented minds. It holds workshops to find these abilities, then trains them afterward under Ganit Guru's direction and supervision. With this goal, a new venture has been started for underprivileged students in Saharsa, Bihar, India. We have the know-how to produce math whizzes. If you have the brain, we can train! Ganit Guru, the founder of Ganit Math, a novel approach to mathematics education, is an expert in the subject and is a TEACHERS' TEACHER. He is regarded as India's Ramanujan of the modern age.
